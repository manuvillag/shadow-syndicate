## üìç OUTPOSTS

### Tier 1: Starter Outposts (Level 1-3)

#### Trading Post Alpha
- **Type**: Trading Post
- **Base Income**: 500 credits/hour
- **Price**: 5,000 credits
- **Level Requirement**: 1
- **Description**: A bustling black-market bazaar orbiting a derelict moon. Smugglers, fixers, and rogue merchants trade everything from antique weapons to stolen corporate data. The station's corridors echo with whispered deals and the clatter of credits changing hands. Perfect for establishing your first foothold in the syndicate network.
- **Upgrade Path**: Increases trading volume and unlocks rare item discounts
- **Special**: Small discount on item purchases (5% per level)

#### Mining Station Beta
- **Type**: Mining Station
- **Base Income**: 750 credits/hour
- **Price**: 7,500 credits
- **Level Requirement**: 1
- **Description**: Automated drills chew through asteroid belts, guarded by underpaid security crews and overworked drones. The station's hull is pockmarked with micrometeorite impacts, and the air smells of ozone and metal shavings. Perfect cover for siphoning rare alloys and running contraband through the ore shipments.
- **Upgrade Path**: Increases alloy production and unlocks alloy-to-credits conversion
- **Special**: Generates alloy resources passively (10/hour per level)

#### Research Lab Gamma
- **Type**: Research Lab
- **Base Income**: 1,000 credits/hour
- **Price**: 10,000 credits
- **Level Requirement**: 1
- **Description**: A sterile, high-security facility where fringe scientists test experimental tech and bio-mods far from regulators' eyes. The lab's white walls are occasionally stained with something that glows faintly in the dark. Unlock cutting-edge equipment and experimental modifications here.
- **Upgrade Path**: Increases research output and unlocks rare tech items
- **Special**: Chance to unlock rare gadgets/boosters (2% per level)

### Tier 2: Mid-Game Outposts (Level 3-5)

#### Manufacturing Hub
- **Type**: Manufacturing
- **Base Income**: 1,500 credits/hour
- **Price**: 15,000 credits
- **Level Requirement**: 3
- **Description**: A sprawling orbital factory line stamping out drones, weapons, and counterfeit corporate gear 24/7. The constant hum of machinery never stops, and the workers are indistinguishable from the machines they operate. Control the means of production and flood the market with your own branded equipment.
- **Upgrade Path**: Increases production capacity and unlocks custom item creation
- **Special**: Converts alloy into credits passively (1:10 ratio per level)

#### Data Center Delta
- **Type**: Data Center
- **Base Income**: 2,000 credits/hour
- **Price**: 20,000 credits
- **Level Requirement**: 3
- **Description**: Racks of quantum servers humming in the dark, filled with corporate secrets, identity shards, and blackmail material. The air is cold and dry, and the only light comes from blinking server status indicators. Information is power, and this is where you store it.
- **Upgrade Path**: Increases data processing and unlocks intelligence contracts
- **Special**: Boosts contract payouts (+10% per level) and hack-type missions

#### Smuggler's Dock "Midnight Pier"
- **Type**: Dock / Port
- **Base Income**: 1,200 credits/hour
- **Price**: 12,000 credits
- **Level Requirement**: 2
- **Description**: A shadowy section of a civilian station where ships dock with transponders off and manifests falsified. The dockmaster takes a cut of every shipment, but asks no questions. Perfect for moving contraband and avoiding customs inspections.
- **Upgrade Path**: Increases smuggling capacity and reduces detection risk
- **Special**: Bonus income when running smuggling contracts (+25% per level)

### Tier 3: Advanced Outposts (Level 5-7)

#### Syndicate Safehouse "Obsidian Nest"
- **Type**: Safehouse
- **Base Income**: 1,800 credits/hour
- **Price**: 18,000 credits
- **Level Requirement**: 5
- **Description**: Hidden bolt-hole laced with dead drops, armories, and emergency medical pods. The walls are reinforced, the exits are numerous, and the location is known only to trusted operatives. When things go wrong, this is where you disappear.
- **Upgrade Path**: Increases security and unlocks advanced facilities
- **Special**: Passive health regeneration (+1 HP/hour per level) and reduced arrest/heat chance

#### Corpus Tower Annex
- **Type**: Corporate Front
- **Base Income**: 2,500 credits/hour
- **Price**: 25,000 credits
- **Level Requirement**: 5
- **Description**: A "legitimate" corporate office controlled through shell companies, used to launder credits and influence officials. The building looks like any other corporate tower, but the top floors are reserved for syndicate business. Blend in with the suits and profit from the inside.
- **Upgrade Path**: Increases corporate influence and unlocks special contracts
- **Special**: High credit income and unlocks special corp-side contracts

#### Junkworld Salvage Yard
- **Type**: Scrap / Salvage
- **Base Income**: 1,000 credits/hour
- **Price**: 10,000 credits
- **Level Requirement**: 4
- **Description**: Mountains of wrecked ships and drones piled under a toxic sky, where desperate crews strip anything of value. The yard is a maze of twisted metal and scavenger camps. One person's trash is another's treasure, and you're the one making the profit.
- **Upgrade Path**: Increases salvage efficiency and unlocks rare finds
- **Special**: Generates random items/components (1 per day per level)

### Tier 4: Elite Outposts (Level 7+)

#### Void Monastery
- **Type**: Religious Enclave
- **Base Income**: 3,000 credits/hour
- **Price**: 50,000 credits
- **Level Requirement**: 7
- **Description**: An ancient station occupied by "ascetics" who worship the void‚Äîand quietly deal in forbidden tech. The monastery's halls are silent except for the hum of meditation chambers and the occasional sound of credits changing hands. Mystical and profitable.
- **Upgrade Path**: Increases mystical resources and unlocks unique abilities
- **Special**: Rare, high-risk events with unique rewards and penalties

#### Quantum Forge Station
- **Type**: Advanced Manufacturing
- **Base Income**: 4,000 credits/hour
- **Price**: 75,000 credits
- **Level Requirement**: 10
- **Description**: A cutting-edge facility that uses quantum entanglement to manufacture impossible weapons and equipment. The station's core glows with contained singularities, and the products it creates shouldn't exist according to known physics. But they do, and they're yours.
- **Upgrade Path**: Unlocks legendary-tier equipment crafting
- **Special**: Can craft legendary items and weapons

### Tier 1: Street Level (Level 1-10)

#### Scarlet Viper
- **Faction**: Street Syndicate
- **Power Level Range**: 5-15
- **Rewards**: 750-2,250 credits, 30-90 XP
- **Adrenal Cost**: 5-8
- **Description**: Knife-fighter turned enforcer, known for leaving a crimson calling card at every scene. Prefers close-quarters ambushes in cramped corridors. Her movements are fluid and deadly, like a serpent striking. She's killed more people with a blade than most have with guns.
- **Combat Style**: High burst damage, weak to ranged/tech attacks
- **Loot**: Common weapons, street-level gear

#### Neon Ghost
- **Faction**: Net-Runner Collective
- **Power Level Range**: 8-18
- **Rewards**: 1,200-2,700 credits, 48-108 XP
- **Adrenal Cost**: 6-9
- **Description**: A hacker whose physical body is rarely seen; operates through hijacked drones and holo-doppelg√§ngers. When you fight Neon Ghost, you're not sure if you're fighting the real person or a projection. Their attacks come from everywhere and nowhere at once.

#### Shadow Flame
- **Faction**: Pyro Syndicate
- **Power Level Range**: 10-20
- **Rewards**: 1,500-3,000 credits, 60-120 XP

- **Rewards**: 2,250-4,500 credits, 90-180 XP

- **Adrenal Cost**: 10-15

#### Quantum Blade

- **Power Level Range**: 25-40
- **Rewards**: 3,750-6,000 credits, 150-240 XP
- **Adrenal Cost**: 12-18
- **Description**: A celebrity killer-for-hire fighting for reputation as much as credits, livestreaming some duels to the undernet. They fight with style and flair, making every kill a performance. Their blade cuts through reality itself.
- **Combat Style**: High crit chance, telegraphed but deadly strikes
- **Loot**: Legendary weapons, reputation items

#### "Package Delivery"
- **Difficulty**: Easy
- **Energy Cost**: 10
- **Credits Reward**: 500-800
- **XP Reward**: 25-40
- **Loot Chance**: 10%
- **Description**: A simple courier job. Pick up a package from Point A, deliver it to Point B. No questions asked. The client pays well for discretion. Perfect for beginners looking to make their first credits.

#### "Data Retrieval"
- **Difficulty**: Easy
- **Energy Cost**: 12
- **Credits Reward**: 600-900
- **XP Reward**: 30-45
- **Loot Chance**: 15%
- **Description**: Break into a low-security corporate terminal and download some files. The security is minimal, but the data is valuable. A good way to learn the basics of hacking and infiltration.

#### "Debt Collection"
- **Difficulty**: Medium
- **Energy Cost**: 15
- **Credits Reward**: 800-1,200
- **XP Reward**: 40-60

- **Description**: Someone owes money, and you're collecting it. Sometimes they pay willingly, sometimes they don't. Either way, you get paid. A straightforward job with a bit of risk.

- **Rarity**: Legendary
- **Type**: Weapon
- **Attack Boost**: +120
- **Special**: 25% crit + AoE

- **Rarity**: Common
- **Type**: Weapon

## üë• CREW ROLES

### Enforcer
- **Description**: Combat specialists trained in weapons and tactics. They're your muscle, your protection, and your first line of defense. Enforcers excel at combat operations and can turn the tide of any fight.

- **Specialization**: Hacking, Data Extraction, System Manipulation

### Smuggler
- **Description**: Logistics experts who know how to move contraband, avoid customs, and turn a profit on the black market. They're your connection to the underground economy, and they make money even when you're not working.
- **Bonus**: +18% Resource Income (requires 4+ Smugglers)
